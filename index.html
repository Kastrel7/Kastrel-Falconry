Come visit us for an exclusive falconry experience here in Killarney. Why not seize your opportunity to take part in “the sport of kings”. This experience will open a whole new world into an ancient art, thought to be over 4000 years old.

Kastrel Falconry is located in Killarney, Co. Kerry. Based less than 2 miles from Killarney town centre. At Kastrel Falconry, we keep several species of falcons of prey including kestrels, peregrines and gyrfalcons. All of these birds are captive reared and can only be owned by a licensed falconer.

Traditionally, Falconry was practiced solely for hunting to catch food. While our falcons do hunt, a falcon walk or falconry experience is more about experiencing the indescribable, while interacting with these majestic birds.

At Kastrel Falconry, you will be entertained and hopefully also educated about falcons all over the world, their importance in the ecosystem here in Killarney, and also throughout history, hunting together with humans. Call us or send an email to arrange an appointment, where you get a personal encounter with some of our falcons. We fly our falcons on a working, traditional Irish farm.

Our Premises is not open to the public, private hawk walks/displays are arranged by contacting us to make an appointment.
